Travels

I have had the immense fortune to be able to travel to 49 countries (and counting!). The objective of this website is to help people learn about countries and cultures they otherwise might never discover, using my travel experience to make their history more accessible than your standard history textbook. Each country's page has 7 paragraphs, organized in the following structure:

  • Paragraph 1: An introduction to my experience in the country.
  • Paragraph 2: A summary of the city (or cities) I visited, with some historical background.
  • Paragraphs 3-4: Two background paragraphs about the country's history and/or geopolitics.
  • Paragraphs 5-7: Three paragraphs, each putting a picture I took during my visit into historical and/or geographical context.

DISCLAIMER: My intention is for this platform to serve as a learning tool, so I frequently tackle obscure historical situations that are not easy to grasp. Most of the information I cover is from common knowledge verified with general online sources. I do not claim to be an expert, so if you catch any inaccuracies in my writing, please let me know so that I can change it. Also, I have tailored my writing to my network, which is based in the U.S. or in Western Europe, so keep that in mind when reading (that being said I do try my best to remain neutral and accessible to people from all countries).
